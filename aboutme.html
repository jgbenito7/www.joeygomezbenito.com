About Me

I grew up in Simi Valley, a town in Southern California. I attended Royal High School for four years and graduated in 2012 with plans to major in Computer Science at UCLA. I am half Spanish, I am bilingual in Spanish and English, and I have been traveling all of my life to Spain.

Volleyball

I am a member of the UCLA Men's Club Volleyball Team and have been Outside Hitter for the past two years. I am also the captain of the Beta Theta Pi Intramural Volleyball Team and am in charge of letting the team know when games are and encouraging everyone to participate.

Fun
